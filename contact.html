Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Uttarakhand?

Spring (Mar-May) and Autumn (Sep-Oct) are ideal. Summer is good for adventure activities. Winter is perfect for skiing at Auli.

How do I book a package?

Fill out our booking form above or contact us directly via phone/email. Our team will assist with customization and confirmation.

What is included in a package?

Accommodation, meals, guided tours, activities, entrance fees, and transportation are typically included. Check individual packages for details.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not included by default but we recommend adding it. We can assist with insurance options at booking.
