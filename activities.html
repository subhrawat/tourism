Adventure Activities

Experience Thrilling Adventures in the Himalayas

Popular Activities

Choose from a variety of adventure experiences

Trekking

Explore mountain trails with scenic views. From beginner-friendly walks to challenging high-altitude treks like Chopta Trek and Auli Trek.

Best Season: Sep-Oct, Mar-May

River Rafting

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting on Ganges and Brahmaputra rivers. Suitable for both beginners and adventure seekers.

Best Season: Oct-Nov, Feb-May

Paragliding

Soar above the mountains and experience breathtaking aerial views. Popular sites include Bir Billing and Munsiyari.

Best Season: Sep-Oct, Mar-May

Skiing

Experience world-class skiing at Auli, India's premier ski destination with runs for all skill levels.

Best Season: Dec-Feb

Rock Climbing

Climb scenic rock formations and learn professional climbing techniques with certified instructors.

Best Season: Year-round

Horseback Riding

Explore mountain trails on horseback. Guided tours through scenic valleys and alpine meadows available.

Best Season: Mar-Oct

Photography Tours

Capture stunning landscapes and wildlife. Professional guided tours for both amateurs and experienced photographers.

Best Season: All year

Camping & Bonfire

Experience nature up close with camping under starry skies. Includes bonfires, campfire meals, and outdoor activities.

Best Season: Mar-Oct

Safety Guidelines

Physical Fitness

Ensure you are in good physical condition before attempting any adventure activity.

Proper Equipment

Use certified and well-maintained equipment. Always wear safety gear as required.

Professional Guides

Always hire certified guides and instructors for adventure activities.

Weather Conditions

Check weather forecasts and avoid activities during adverse weather conditions.

Medical Check-up

Consult with a doctor before high-altitude activities, especially for altitude sickness.

Travel Insurance

Always carry comprehensive travel and adventure insurance.
