Popular Destinations

Nainital

A picturesque hill station with the famous Naini Lake, surrounded by snow-capped mountains. Perfect for boating, trekking, and enjoying nature.

Elevation: 2,084m

Auli

India's premier ski destination with breathtaking Alpine views. Experience winter sports, skiing, and adventure at 3,000m altitude.

Elevation: 3,000m

Chopta

The Mini Switzerland of India with dense forests and rolling meadows. Ideal for camping, trekking, and experiencing untouched nature.

Elevation: 2,600m

Kedarnath

One of the most sacred pilgrimage sites dedicated to Lord Shiva. Home to the ancient Kedarnath Temple at 3,583m altitude in the Himalayas.

Elevation: 3,583m

Badrinath

Sacred shrine of Lord Vishnu surrounded by mountains. Known for its natural hot water springs and spiritual significance in Hindu tradition.

Elevation: 3,300m

Rishikesh

Yoga capital of India, situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River. Famous for yoga ashrams, meditation centers, and adventure sports.

Elevation: 340m

Mussoorie

Queen of hills offering adventure activities, scenic cable car rides, and picturesque trekking trails with panoramic views of the plains.

Elevation: 2,005m

Corbett National Park

India's first national park known for tiger conservation and wildlife. Experience jungle safaris and spot diverse flora and fauna.

Area: 520 sq km

Haridwar

Gateway to the Gods where the sacred Ganges flows into the plains. Major pilgrimage and adventure destination with river rafting opportunities.

Elevation: 340m
